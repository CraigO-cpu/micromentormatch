About Us

Inspired by a need for simple, human support, MicroMentor Match connects people with volunteer mentors for fast, practical solutions. Whether it’s a tech hiccup or workplace stress, we’re here to help you move forward.

FAQ

How does it work? Submit your question via the “Ask a Question” tab, and we’ll match you with a mentor within 24 hours.
Is it free? Yes, our mentors volunteer their time.
What problems can I ask about? Small issues like WordPress errors, resume tips, or stress management.
No response? We’ll let you know within 48 hours if no mentor is available.
How do I mentor? Sign up via the “Become a Mentor” tab.
